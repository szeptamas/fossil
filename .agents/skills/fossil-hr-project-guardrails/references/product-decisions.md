# Product decisions

Current approved direction:

- This repository contains original Lion/Fossil HR applications and supporting infrastructure.
- Upstream repositories remain clean references.
- The first original app is `lionHelloApp`.
- Applications initially remain independent.
- Shared abstractions require evidence from at least two apps.
- Real-watch behavior is the final source of truth.
- The raw knowledge archive stays outside Git.
- Distilled reusable conclusions belong in `docs\` or `knowledge\`.
